Our Services

Our team at Amare HC Support Services provides a range of home care services designed to meet the diverse needs of our clients. From daily assistance to specialized support, we are here to help you and your loved ones thrive at home.

Care at Home

We provide essential in-home support, assisting with tasks such as personal care, meal preparation, and medication management. Our caregivers work alongside each client, enabling them to live independently and comfortably in their own home.

24-Hour Live-in Care

Our live-in care services provide round-the-clock support and companionship, offering families peace of mind knowing that their loved one has the constant care they need. Our live-in caregivers are trained to provide both physical and emotional support, ensuring a high quality of life.

Respite Care

We understand the demands placed on primary caregivers and offer temporary respite care to give families a much-needed break. Our skilled caregivers step in to provide comprehensive care, allowing family members to recharge, confident that their loved one is in safe hands.

End-of-Life Care

Our end-of-life care focuses on providing comfort, dignity, and emotional support during difficult times. With a compassionate and sensitive approach, our caregivers help clients and their families navigate the end-of-life journey with respect and empathy.
