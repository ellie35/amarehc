About Us

Amare HC Support Services is dedicated to providing high-quality home care services that meet the individual needs of each client. Based in Larbert-Falkirk, our team consists of highly experienced healthcare professionals, including a Registered Mental Health Nurse and a Registered Occupational Therapist with over 20 years of expertise.

Our Services

Care at Home

Our caregivers provide support with daily tasks, helping clients maintain their independence while receiving the assistance they need in their own homes.

24-Hour Live-in Care

We offer full-time live-in care to ensure that clients have support and companionship around the clock.

Respite Care

Our respite care services allow primary caregivers to take a break while knowing that their loved ones are in safe and capable hands.

End-of-Life Care

Our compassionate end-of-life care focuses on comfort, respect, and dignity during difficult times.
